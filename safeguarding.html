What is Safeguarding?

“Safeguarding” refers to measures designed to protect the health, wellbeing and human rights of individuals.These measures allow children, young people and adults to live free from abuse, harm and neglect. Every organisation, small group and individual should be aware of their responsibility in work and society.

Safeguarding Video

Next Step is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all members. We have a responsibility to safeguard and promote the welfare of all members and to ensure that all members are protected from harm.

Our safeguarding policy is based on the principles of:

  • Empowerment
  • Prevention
  • Proportionality
  • Protection
  • Partnership

Our safeguarding policy is in place to ensure that all members are protected from harm and that appropriate action is taken to address any concerns about the welfare of a member.

If you have any concerns about the welfare of a member, please contact us or report the issue to Sheffield City Council's
